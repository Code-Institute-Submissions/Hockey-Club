Mens Second

Castlebar Mens Second have had an excellent few seasons. Following a re-building period, the Second Team have been promoted the past seasons successively. This means a return to South Premier hockey for the first time in several seasons.

The Second Team owe much of this success to the many young players that have come through Castlebar junior section in the last few years. Much of this years squad are products of this system and with the addition of some fresh blood, Castlebar have a strong squad. The Second Team will look to consolidate their position in Western Division 2 this season and build for the future.
